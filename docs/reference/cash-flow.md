# Cash Flow

The Cash Flow section provides a comprehensive view of your income and expenses over time, allowing you to track your savings rate and understand where your money is going.

## Yearly and Monthly Views

You can view your cash flow on a yearly or monthly basis. The views display the total income, expenses, and savings for the selected period.

### Monthly Breakdown Widget

The Monthly Breakdown widget offers a multi-currency view of your cash flow for a specific month. It aggregates your income and expenses to give you an accurate picture of your financial health across different currencies.

## Income Statement

The Income Statement provides a detailed breakdown of your revenue and expenses for a given period. It correctly accounts for liabilities and calculates starting and ending balances directly from the balance sheet (assets at market value + liabilities at book value), mirroring the Networth page for the current year. This ensures that the Income Statement accurately handles liability-funded transactions and foreign currency mark-to-market.

## 30-Day Spending Trends

The **Monthly Expenses** page now shows a **30-Day Spending Trends**
grid below the expense calendar.  For each expense category, the grid
displays:

- The **current 30-day total** (today − 30 days to today).
- The **previous 30-day total** (today − 60 days to today − 30 days).
- The **variance** amount and a colour-coded percentage change
  indicator: red for an increase (↑) and green for a decrease (↓).
- A **6-month sparkline** bar chart showing per-category monthly
  history.  The current month's bar is highlighted in the category
  colour; a dashed red average line is overlaid.

`Expenses:Tax` transactions are excluded from all trend calculations.

## Monthly Cashflow Widget

The dashboard includes a **Monthly Cashflow** widget that provides a
multi-currency cashflow breakdown for the current month, showing total
income versus total expenses using the original ledger quantities
(without mark-to-market conversion).  This gives a quick, currency-
accurate view of your net cashflow for the month at a glance.
